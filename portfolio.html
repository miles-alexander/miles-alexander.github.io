Mass Shootings in the United States: Patterns and Insights

Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) on multiple U.S. mass shooting datasets using R, dplyr, and ggplot2 to investigate demographic, geographic, and behavioral patterns. Cleaned and standardized real-world data, generated statistical summaries and visualizations, and assessed relationships between mental health indicators, weapon types, race, gender, and incident locations while documenting data quality limitations and opportunities for future predictive modeling.

Tesla News Sentiment and Stock Price Analysis

Developed an NLP-driven financial analytics pipeline to investigate the relationship between news sentiment and Tesla's short-term stock performance. Leveraged VADER sentiment analysis, integrated NewsAPI and Yahoo Finance datasets, performed data cleaning and feature engineering, conducted Pearson correlation analysis and statistical hypothesis testing, and explored time-series trends to evaluate whether media sentiment could predict stock price movements.

Predicting Seoul Bike Rental Demand Based on Weather and Temporal Factors

Built an end-to-end demand forecasting pipeline using Python and machine learning to predict hourly bike rental demand. Performed exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, data preprocessing, and model development while comparing Linear Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and ARIMA forecasting techniques. Evaluated predictive performance using RSME and MAE to identify operational insights for bike-sharing resource optimization.
