Weather Program

A command-line application that provides current weather information for U.S. cities using the OpenWeatherMap API. Users can search by city/state or ZIP code and receive real-time details like temperature, humidity, pressure, and conditions.

Mass Shootings in the United States: Patterns and Insights

This project analyzes mass shooting incidents in the U.S. to uncover trends related to weapon types, locations, mental health, and demographics. It aims to support prevention strategies and policy discussions using data science techniques.

RAFIKI – Guest Feedback Tracking & Reporting Tool

A PowerApps and SharePoint-based tool developed to collect, track, and report Guest feedback at Magic Kingdom. It supports mobile and desktop entry, real-time categorization, and operational insights for follow-up.

Hate Crime in America: State-Level Analysis (2019)

This project visualizes and analyzes 2019 hate crime data across U.S. states to reveal geographic and categorical patterns. It seeks to inform policy, prevention strategies, and community-focused solutions.

MLS Salary Analysis: Performance vs. Compensation

A comparative analysis of Major League Soccer player performance and salary data to evaluate the fairness of pay across roles and positions based on goals, assists, saves, and other performance metrics.

Career Assistant Chatbot

An AI-powered career development chatbot built with Streamlit and OpenAI’s fine-tuned GPT-3.5. It delivers real-time, customized advice on career paths, skills to acquire, and relevant certifications.
